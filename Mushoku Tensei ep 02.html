Rudy begins training with his new magic tutor and learning swordsmanship from his father. He makes rapid progress, but traumatic memories from his past life leave him too afraid to go outside. Is his second chance already doomed to failure?

  • By: Studio Bind

The fans of ‘Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation’ or ‘Jobless Reincarnation: I Will Seriously Try If I Go to Another World’ are extremely hyped up and excited to find out that the animated series will be receiving a second cour in the fall of this year! As we already know, ‘Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation’ is originally a light novel series which was written by the great Rifujin Na Magonote. The light novel was initially published online, over the internet. On a worldwide accessible novel website called ‘Shosetsuka Ni Naro’. The novel was available for the fans to read online from the November of 2012. After a year, ‘Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation’ started getting printed in the ‘Media Factory’s MF Books’ company, for which the illustrations were done by Shirotaka.
